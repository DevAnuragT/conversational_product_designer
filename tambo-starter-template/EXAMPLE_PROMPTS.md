## üí° Tips for Writing Effective Prompts

### ‚úÖ Good Prompt Structure
- **Be specific** about the business/product name
- **Mention desired components** (hero, features, pricing, CTA)
- **Include content details** (number of features, pricing tiers)
- **Specify the tone** (professional, friendly, modern, etc.)

### ‚ùå Avoid These Patterns
- Vague descriptions without specifics
- Requesting components not in the registry
- Overly complex requirements in a single prompt
- Missing key information like business name or purpose

### üéØ Pro Tips
- Start with simpler prompts to test the system
- Be specific about numbers (3 features, 4 pricing tiers)
- Include the business context for better content generation
- Mention the target audience when relevant
